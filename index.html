Our Story

CULTURE
BECOMES
CLOTHING.

We grew up watching the Rath Yatra processions, hearing the beat of dhol at village fairs, tracing our fingers over Sambalpuri weaves on our grandmothers' sarees. That identity — vivid, proud, deeply rooted — had no place in mainstream fashion.

So we made one. SKWIRREL was born to transform Odisha's culture, language, music, and memory into wearable fashion. Every piece tells a story you can carry with you.

Sustainability

FROM NATURE.
MADE TO WEAR.

We're developing a fabric made from wood pulp and cotton. A smarter, cleaner, more mindful way to create what you wear.

✦

Wood Pulp

Renewable. Responsibly sourced. Naturally strong. We work with certified forestry partners to ensure our pulp is harvested with respect for the earth.

✦

Cotton

Natural. Breathable. Timeless comfort. Our cotton is ethically sourced from Indian farmers who share our commitment to quality and responsibility.

Root Stories

Rooted In

THE SOUL
OF ODISHA

01

Rath Yatra — The Chariot Festival

One of the world's greatest gatherings. We carry its spirit of movement, devotion, and togetherness into every stitch.

02

Sambalpuri Weaves

Ikat patterns born in the looms of western Odisha. Ancient geometry reinterpreted for contemporary streetwear.

03

Odissi Dance

The sculptural grace of one of India's oldest classical dance forms. Fluidity and strength woven into our design language.

04

Coastal Heritage

Puri's shores, Chilika's waters, and the fishermen's boats — the rhythms of coastal life shape our palette and patterns.
